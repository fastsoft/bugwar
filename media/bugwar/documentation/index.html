Introduction

Greetings, maggot. Welcome to the Mechanized Infantry.

You've left the the privileged life of a normal citizen, a life where software works properly the majority of the time, a life protected by the sweat and toil of the developers in the trenches. You know what it's like to fight a bug on your own, with primitive tools, but here in the MI, we bring bug bashing to the next level.

Warfare is about information, not brute strength. You've no doubt sallied forth like Custer, squashing one bug, only to have two more counterattack at your flank. That's not the way to fight. When you put your enemy down, you make sure he stays down. Welcome to the power of coordinated regression. Welcome to Bugwar.
